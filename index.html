Kellye's Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps to Make Grilled Cheese

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top o bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
